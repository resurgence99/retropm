The Game Of Eleven

June 9th, 2021. Bengaluru

The day started with the whistle of the train, puffing briskly towards the rain prone woods. The shadow casted by the sky towering trees was enough to cover the entire forest floor with its enchanting fear. In my opinion, it was nearly impossible for anyone to know the presence of any settlements from a distance. The mystical atmosphere was enough to put even a gloomy man to complete enthusiasm in just a sight. The trees for over hundred years had instilled fear among the young. It was in this dense precipitous forest a young vibrant and valor soldier was born. Continue Reading...
